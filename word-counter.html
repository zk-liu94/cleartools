Why Word Count Matters for English Learners

For non-native English speakers, tracking word count and sentence length helps you write more clearly. Short sentences are easier to understand, and knowing your average word and sentence length helps you adjust your writing style for your audience.

Try to keep your average sentence length under 20 words for better readability. Use shorter words when possible to make your writing clearer for a global audience.

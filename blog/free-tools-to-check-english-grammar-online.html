Best Free Tools to Check English Grammar Online

June 25, 2026

Finding a good free grammar checker is essential for non-native English speakers. Here are the best free tools available online to check your English grammar.

1. ClearWrite Grammar Checker

Our Grammar Checker is completely free and designed for English learners. It checks for subject-verb agreement, article usage, commonly confused words, double negatives, and common phrase errors. No sign-up required.

2. ClearWrite Readability Checker

The Readability Checker uses multiple formulas to score your text. While it is not a grammar checker, it helps you see if your sentences are clear and easy to understand.

3. LanguageTool

LanguageTool is an open-source grammar checker that supports over 25 languages. Its free version catches grammar, spelling, and style errors. It also has a browser extension for real-time checking.

4. Hemingway Editor

The Hemingway Editor highlights complex sentences and suggests simpler alternatives. It is excellent for improving readability alongside grammar checks.

5. How to Use Grammar Tools Effectively

Start by writing your text. Then run it through our Grammar Checker to catch common issues. Next, use the Word Counter to check sentence length. Finally, use the Readability Checker to ensure your writing is clear and easy to read.

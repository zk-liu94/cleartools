Best Free Word Counter Tools for Students

June 25, 2026

Word counters are essential tools for students who need to meet word count requirements for essays, assignments, and reports. Here are the best free word counter tools available.

1. ClearWrite Word Counter

Our Word Counter gives you instant stats on words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, and lines. It also shows average word length and sentence length. Perfect for students who need to track their writing progress.

2. ClearWrite Word Frequency Counter

The Word Frequency Counter shows which words you use most often. This helps students identify overused words and expand their vocabulary for better essays.

3. ClearWrite Syllable Counter

Our Syllable Counter shows syllable counts for each word. Students can use this to understand word complexity and improve readability in their academic writing.

4. Tips for Meeting Word Counts

  • Set a daily writing goal and use a word counter to track progress.
  • Write your first draft without worrying about word count.
  • Use the Readability Checker to ensure your writing is clear and readable.
  • Check your average sentence length with the Word Counter.

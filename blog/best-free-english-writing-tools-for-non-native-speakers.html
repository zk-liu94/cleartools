Best Free English Writing Tools for Non-Native Speakers in 2026

June 25, 2026

As a non-native English speaker, you do not need expensive software to improve your writing. Free online tools can help you check your word count, readability, word frequency, and more. Here are the best free tools available in 2026.

1. ClearWrite Word Counter

Our Word Counter gives you instant stats on words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, and lines. It also shows average word length and sentence length, which helps you track your writing style over time.

2. ClearWrite Readability Checker

The Readability Checker uses Flesch Reading Ease, Gunning Fog, and Coleman-Liau formulas to score your text. It tells you the grade level of your writing and gives you tips to improve clarity.

3. ClearWrite Syllable Counter

Our Syllable Counter shows the syllable count for each word in your text. This helps you identify complex words that may be hard for your readers to understand.

4. ClearWrite Text Case Converter

The Text Case Converter instantly transforms your text between uppercase, lowercase, title case, and sentence case. Useful for formatting headings and titles correctly.

5. ClearWrite Word Frequency Counter

Our Word Frequency Counter shows which words you use most often. This is excellent for identifying overused words and expanding your vocabulary.

6. ClearWrite Text Diff Checker

The Text Diff Checker compares two versions of text and highlights what changed. Perfect for reviewing your revisions and tracking your editing progress.

Other Useful Free Tools

  • Grammarly: Grammar and spell checking with browser integration.
  • Hemingway Editor: Highlights complex sentences and suggests simplifications.
  • ProWritingAid: In-depth writing analysis with style suggestions.
  • Google Docs: Free word processor with built-in spell check and collaboration.

All of ClearWrite tools are completely free and designed specifically for English learners. Start using them now to improve your English writing today.

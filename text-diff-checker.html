Compare two versions of your English text to see what changed. Green highlights show added text, red shows removed text. Useful for tracking edits and revisions.

How to Use Text Diff for English Learning

Paste your original draft in the left box and your revised version in the right box. The tool highlights what you added (green) and removed (red). This helps you see your editing patterns and learn from your revisions.

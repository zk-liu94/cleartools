What This Grammar Checker Checks

This tool looks for common English grammar mistakes that non-native speakers often make:

  • Subject-verb agreement — "He go" should be "He goes"
  • Commonly confused words — Your/You're, There/Their/They're, Its/It's, Then/Than
  • A vs An usage — "A apple" should be "An apple"
  • Double negatives — "Don't have nothing" should be "Don't have anything"
  • Common phrase errors — "Different than" should be "Different from"

How to Use the Readability Score

The Flesch Reading Ease score ranges from 0 to 100. Higher scores mean easier reading:

  • 90-100 — Very Easy. Suitable for young readers.
  • 80-89 — Easy. Conversational English.
  • 70-79 — Fairly Easy. Good for general audiences.
  • 60-69 — Standard. Plain English for most readers.
  • 50-59 — Fairly Difficult. High school level.
  • 30-49 — Difficult. College level.
  • 0-29 — Very Confusing. Academic or technical.

Tip for English learners: Aim for a readability score of 60-70 if you are writing for a general audience. Use shorter sentences and simpler words to improve your score.
